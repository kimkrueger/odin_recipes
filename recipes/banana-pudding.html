Banana Pudding

This delectable dessert is made of bananas, vanilla wafers, and milk.

Layer by layer, the banana pudding is formed when pudding mix and milk is mixed.
Following a layer of condensed milk and vanilla.

All the while vanilla wafers on the side to give it a nice crunch.

Finally, topping it with banana whipped custard with banana slices.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Pudding mix
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Vanilla
  • Frozen whipped topping
  • Vanilla wafers
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place milk and pudding mix in a large bowl; beat with a whisk for 2 minutes. Blend in condensed milk until smooth.
  3. Stir in vanilla, then fold in whipped topping.
  4. Layer wafers, banana slices, and pudding mixture in a glass serving bowl.
  5. For best results, chill pudding in the refrigerator for at least an hour before serving. When ready to serve top with extra crushed wafers.
  6. Enjoy!
